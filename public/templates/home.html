OUR MISSION IS TO BRIDGE THE TUITION FINANCE GAP FOR STUDENTS PLANNING TO ATTEND A PROGRAMMING BOOTCAMP.

We believe in the viability of programming bootcamps as a vehicle for self-empowerment in today's workforce. However, these schools often lack FAFSA support, leaving many potential applicants with few options for covering tuition costs.

codefund is a community of like-minded individuals working on the solution.
